HORTICULTURE

The Department of Horticulture plays a pivotal role in advancing the science and practice of horticultural crop production and management. As a specialized branch of agricultural sciences, the department is dedicated to the study, research, and development of various horticultural disciplines including fruit science (pomology), vegetable science (olericulture), floriculture, medicinal and aromatic plants, plantation crops, and post-harvest technology.The department contributes significantly to the growth of the horticulture sector by fostering innovation, increasing productivity, and enhancing the livelihoods of farming communities. It also plays a role in environmental conservation and urban greening initiatives through its work in floriculture and landscaping.
